A narrative adventure game where you develop a bioweapon on your dead father's order.

Teaser

Noah is an adventure game where the goal is to create a mass-destructive bioweapon. In this narrative-focused experience, players are tasked with obeying a mad scientist’s last orders as his daughter, Abigael, on a small island. Follow his instructions and learn to take care of yourself and your sister. Explore the island, gather herbs, collect gadgets, and experiment with them in your laboratory. Compare the results and finalize your weapon.
